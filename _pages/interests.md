Research is a big part of my life, but not the whole story. Outside work, I like sports and being outdoors: tennis, hiking, skiing and snowboarding, wake surfing, and fishing.

I like activities that have a mix of rhythm, patience, and a little bit of risk. Tennis gives me that fast, technical side; hiking and fishing slow things down; skiing, snowboarding, and wake surfing are where I get to chase weather, water, and mountains.
